Individual Authorized Agent

A P.O. Box holder can entrust any person to receive any incoming postal items delivered to their P.O. Box on their behalf.

 

How to apply:

  • Provide passport photocopy of the Agent with a copy of the resident visa page (for expats).
  • Provide (2) Photos size 4x4.5 cm of the agent.
  • Complete the Agent Authorization Form & signed.

 

Annual Fee:

The annual fee for this service is 50 AED.
