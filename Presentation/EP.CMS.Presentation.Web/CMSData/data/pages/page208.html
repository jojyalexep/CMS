Emirates Post Group is highlighting employment opportunities available for Emirati job-seekers in the Group and its subsidiaries at the 15th National Career Fair, which is being held at the Expo Centre Sharjah from February 20 to 22.

Mr. Fahad Al Hosani, Acting President of Emirates Post Group, said: “Emiratisation is one of our top priorities and we are constantly working to fine-tune our recruitment policies to create more employment opportunities for Emirati job-seekers. We regard this important career fair as a major platform to reach out to Emiratis and introduce them to current and future vacancies in the Emirates Post Group and its subsidiaries.”

Mr. Thani bin Jarsh, CEO of Operations, Emirates Post Group, said: “Emirates Post Group is very pleased to take part in this important career fair. We are supporting the event as part of our involvement in Abser, the initiative launched by the President His Highness Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan with the aim of boosting Emiratisation across diverse sectors. We share the UAE government’s vision of training and employing Emiratis in every aspect of business.”

The National Career Exhibition is an annual event held under the patronage of His Highness Dr. Sheikh Sultan Bin Mohammed Al Qassimi. It is organised by Expo Centre Sharjah with the support of the Sharjah Chamber of Commerce and Industry (SCCI), Human Resources Development Committee in the Banking sector and Emirates Institute for the Banking and Financial Studies.
