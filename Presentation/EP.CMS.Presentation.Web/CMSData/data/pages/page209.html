Emirates Post Group celebrated the victory of UAE Football Team in the 21st Gulf Cup at a function held today in the customer area of Deira Main Post Office, attended by top officials as well as employees and customers.

A cake designed with a photograph of the triumphant football team was cut on the occasion, and distributed to the attendees and post office customers.

Among the top officials who attended the celebrations were Mr. Fahad Al Hosani, Acting President of Emirates Post Group (EPG); Mr. Thani Bin Jarsh, CEO, Operations, EPG; Mr. Ibrahim Bin Karam, Chief Commercial Officer, EPG; Mr. Abdulla Alashram, CEO, Emirates Post and Mr. Sultan Al Midfa, CEO of Empost, and other officials.

“The National team’s victory in the Gulf Cup is a historic occasion which calls for celebrations and festivities,” said Mr. Al Hosani. “It is a matter of great pride for all Nationals as well as expatriates living in the UAE that our National team has defeated some of the region’s biggest national teams. The victory also demonstrates the UAE team has attained very high standards, and the day is not far away when our national team will bring international honours in major events like Olympics and the World Cup.”

“Through this celebration we want to express our support to this great team that has brought such a big honour to the country. We wish them greater success in the months and years to come,” added Al Hosani.
