Only main post offices in the UAE will be open in the morning on Thursday, January 24, 2013, during the holiday to mark the birthday of Prophet Mohammed (PBUH). All the other post offices will remain closed.

The following main post offices will be open from 9 am to 1 pm only:

Abu Dhabi Central Post Office and Hamdan Post Office in Abu Dhabi; Dubai Central (Karama) Post Office, Al Khor Post Office in Dubai and Dubai International Airport; Sharjah Central Post Office, Ghuwair Post Office and Khorfakkan Post Office in Sharjah; Ajman Central Post Office in Ajman; Umm Al Quwain Industrial Post Office in Umm Al Quwain; Ras Al Khaimah Central Post Office and Al Uraibi Post Office in Ras Al Khaimah; Fujairah Central Post Office and Al Ain Central Post Office.

“Emirates Post is pleased to extend greetings to all our customers on this blessed occasion of the birthday of Prophet Mohammed (BPUH). It is an occasion to remind ourselves of the message and teachings of the Holy Prophet and apply them in our daily lives,” said Ibrahim Bin Karam, Chief Commercial Officer, Emirates Post Group.
