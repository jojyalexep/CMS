: Emirates Post Group honoured employees from service category during a boat cruise from Al Boom Tourism Village, in appreciation of their effort and dedication, as part of the ‘Thank You’ campaign launched by His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of UAE and Ruler of Dubai.

The function was attended by Mr. Thani Bin Jersh, CEO Operations, Mr. Sultan Al Midfa, CEO of Empost, Mr. Khalid Shahdour, CEO of Information Technology, Mr. Sami Al Khaja, Executive Director of Human Resources, EPG, Mr. Mubarak Al Shamsi, 2nd Postal Zone Director, Mr. Abdullah Salem Busomnoh, 3rd Postal Zone Director, Mr. Rashid Khamis Zayed, Director of Revenue Department and a number of officials and employees.

On this occasion, HE Fahad Al Hosani, Acting President of Emirates Post Group, in his message said the Group was pleased to respond to the kind initiative of His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum. “The ‘Thank you’ initiative reflects the support and concern of the wise leadership for workers and the desire to protect their rights and recognise their role in social development. It also reflects the Islamic values which require that we should care for every person who provides services to the community, regardless of their category, gender or nationality.

Speaking at the function, Mr. Thani Bin Jersh lauded the humanitarian gesture of His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum.  He thanked the staff for their dedication to work and their contribution to the growth of the organisation, while stressing that excellence lies in teamwork which contributes greatly to social development.

The employees who were honoured expressed their gratitude for this noble initiative which has been embraced by all residents of the UAE, thus reflecting on the high levels of humanitarianism in the country.

The program was in line with the directive of HH Sheikh Mohammed to dedicate his Accession Day celebrations to the general labour category staff, such as domestic workers, janitors, public transportation drivers, construction workers, technicians, office support workers and others in different categories.
