Emirates Post Group has signed an agreement with Al Tanmyah Services LLC for supplying office boys and pantry staff to the Group and its subsidiaries.

The agreement was signed by Mr. Fahad Al Hosani, Acting President of Emirates Post Group and Mr. Ali Khalil Al Sayegh, General Manager of Al Tanmyah Services at the EPG head office.

Under the agreement, Al Tanmyah Services will supply trained office boys and pantry staff to EPG and its subsidiaries, five days a week, on an eight-hour shift. The contract is initially valid for one year, to be extended on an annual basis.

“Emirates Post Group is keen to improve these non-core services by outsourcing them to professional companies within the parameters and guidelines of the group,” said Mr. Fahad Al Hosani. “We are pleased to sign this agreement with Al Tanmyah Services and we are confident the company will provide services that meet our expectations.”

Mr. Ali Khalil Al Sayegh commented: “Al Tanmyah Services takes pride in supplying trained and competent staff to companies across the UAE. We ensure that companies don’t have to face the burden of employing and managing the low-category staff, so that the companies can focus on more important functions.  We are confident that we will help Emirates Post Group achieve its objectives in outsourcing these services to us.”
