Based on the vision of His Highness Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, President of the UAE and His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, on enabling the public sector to excel and develop the systems, performance, services and results , Emirates Post Group organized the first induction of ‘ambassadors of excellence’ in the presence of Sultan Al Madfa, CEO of  Empost and Vice Chairman of the Committee and a number of CEOs in addition to the Group’s staff  representatives of  “Ambassador of Excellence ”.

The meeting was opened by Mr. Sultan Al madfa, who welcomed the attendees and praised the Group’s ambassadors of excellence. Also, Amal Bahlouq, Acting Director of  Exellence Department gave a brief on Emirates Award for Excellence in Government Performance and about the participation of Emirates Post Group. This was followed by a detailed explanation about the Group’s participation, and description of the functions of the ambassadors of excellence in addition to brainstorming sessions and competitions that promote and explain the concept of excellence to the audience.

HE Fahad Al Hosani, Acting President of the Emirates Post Group, said that obtaining excellence awards is not intended to create competition among government agencies, but it is a model business style to be followed in the group, and this will only come through continuity in the development of outstanding performance and commitment to the application of and the development of opportunities for improvement.

He expressed his appreciation to all sectors and departments of Emirates Post Group for their efforts to identify opportunities for improvement during the past period, and thanked the CEOs for their continued support for the march of excellence. He praised the ambassadors of excellence involved in the Standing Committee, and urged them to prepare the documents related to participation in Emirates Award for Excellence in Government Performance. He also called on all Emirates Post Group employees to support and cooperate with them to facilitate their work.
