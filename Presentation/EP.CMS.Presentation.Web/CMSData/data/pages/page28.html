Roads & Transport Authority (RTA)

 

1-    Paying Parking Fines

Customers can pay their Roads & Transport Authority parking fines through Emirates Post office across the UAE with a real-time transaction update, for only 5 AED service fee charge.

 

2-    Pre-paid Parking Cards

Pre-Paid Parking Cards are now available at all post-offices across the UAE in the following denominations:

  • 100 AED
  • 50 AED
  • 30 AED

 

3-     Salik Recharge Cards

Salik Recharge Cards are now available at all post-offices across the UAE in the following denominations:

  • 50 AED
  • 100 AED
  • 200 AED
