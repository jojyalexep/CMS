E-Dirham

Emirates Post offices offer E-Dirham services in association with the Ministry of Finance, where customers can buy and top up their E-Dirham card at any of Emirates Post Offices as per the following categories:

  • 50 AED
  • 100 AED
  • 200 AED
  • 300 AED
  • 500 AED
  • 1000 AED
  • 3000 AED
  • 5000 AED
