Sharjah Municipality

 

1- Tenancy Contracts
Sharjah Municipality tenancy contracts can be purchased at any of Emirates Post offices across the UAE, for a value of AED 50.

 

2- Prepaid Parking Cards
Sharjah Municipality’s prepaid parking cards can be purchased at Emirates Post  Offices across the UAE in the denominations:

  • AED 50
  • AED 30
