Dubai Department of Economic Development

Dubai Department of Economic Development (DED) customers can now issue, renew or print a modification of their trade license at any of Emirates Post Offices across the UAE.  Payment permits can also be presented and paid, plus customers can request for a printout of their trade license.

 

How to apply:

  • Provide Renew or cancelation transaction request.
  • Paying the Dubai Department of Economic fees.
  • Paying 15 AED service charge.
