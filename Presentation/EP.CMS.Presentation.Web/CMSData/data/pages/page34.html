Registration of Tenancy Contracts

Emirates Post is offering its customers an easy facility to register the tenancy contracts of Dubai Land Department (without going to real estate brokers) by visiting any of its dedicated post office for this service.

 

How to apply:

  • Fill up the application form online or at a selected post office.
  • Get the following required documents scanned by customer service agent at the counter:

-       Photo copy of the tenancy contract.

-       DEWA Bill (Dubai Electricity & Water Authority).

-       Passport photocopy with the residential visa for non UAE nationals.

-       Photocopy of the Trade License for corporates.

-      Pay AED 195 fee per contract.

 

Post offices providing the service:

-       Main Post Office – Karama

-       Jumeirah Post Office

-       Al Riqqa Post Office

-       Hor Al Anz Post Office

-      Al Rashidiya Post Office
