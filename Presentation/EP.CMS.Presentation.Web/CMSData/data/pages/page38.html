Etisalat

Now customers can enjoy Etisalat services provided in Emirates Post offices across the UAE.

 

1- Pay Telephone Bills

Etisalat telephone bills can be paid at the post office counters.

 

2- Applying for Prepaid Cards Service

Customers can now apply for prepaid card service with the following easy steps:

  • Fill up the Wasel Prepaid application form.
  • Pay 40 AED service fee charge.
  • Provide a copy of the passport and resident visa if applicable.

 

3- Selling Prepaid Cards

Purchasing Prepaid Cards is available at Emirates Post Offices across the UAE in the denominations:

  • 20 AED
  • 25 AED
  • 40 AED
  • 50 AED
  • 100 AED
  • 200 AED
  • 500 AED
