Now customers can enjoy Du services provided in Emirates Post offices across the UAE.

 

1-    Pay Telephone Bills

Du telephone bills can be paid at the post office counters.

 

2-    Applying for Prepaid Cards

  • Fill up the prepaid application form.
  • Pay 55 AED service fee charge.
  • Provide the required documents as when needed.
  • UAE National:  Passport Copy or Emirates ID.
  • GCC National:  Valid GCC Passport or Valid GCC National ID or UAE work permit/Labour card (Issued by Ministry of Labour only).
  • Expatriates:  Valid Passport and valid UAE Visa or Emirates ID or UAE Work Permit/Labour card (Issued by Ministry of Labour Only).
  • Diplomats:  Valid Passport or Emirates ID.

 

 

3-    Selling Prepaid Cards

Selling Prepaid Cards is available at Emirates Post Offices across the UAE in the denominations:

  • 20 AED
  • 50 AED
  • 100 AED
  • 200 AED
