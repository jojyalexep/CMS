Air Arabia

Air Arabia customers can use any of Emirates Post outlets just like any Air Arabia travel shop or travel agent, offering customers as much accessibility and flexibility as possible when purchasing air tickets.

 

How to apply:

  • Customers can book their ticket at the post office or through calling the Customer Service Centre.
  • Pay the value of the ticket.
  • Paying 50 AED per person as a service fee charge, or Pay 10 AED on each booking reference if booking was made by a company other than Emirates Post.
