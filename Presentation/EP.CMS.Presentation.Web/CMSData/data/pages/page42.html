Flydubai

flydubai offer its services through the Emirates Post outlets, very much like as official agent, offering customers accessibility and flexibility for purchasing air tickets.

 

How to apply:

  • Customers can book, change and issue their ticket at the post office or through calling the Customer Service Centre.
  • Pay the value of the ticket.
  • Paying 50 AED per person as a service fee charge.
  • or Pay 30 AED on each booking reference if booking was made by a company other than Emirates Post.
