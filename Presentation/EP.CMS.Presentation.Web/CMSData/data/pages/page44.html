Knowledge Horizon

Emirates Post provides the e-course prepaid cards for the Knowledge Horizon courses which are designed to meet the needs of individuals and companies in English and Arabic.

 

Post offices provide the service:

  • Abu Dhabi:  Main post office, Tourist Club Post Office, Exhibition Centre Post Office.
  • Dubai:  The Central Post Office Karama, Deira Main Post Office, Jumeirah Post Office, Beach Post Office, Airport Post Office, Dubai International Convention Centre.
  • Sharjah:  The Main Post Office and Sharjah University Post Office.
  • Ajman:  The Main Post Office.
  • Umm Al Quwain:  The Main Post Office.
  • Ras Al Khaimah:  The Main Post Office.
  • Fujairah:  The Main Post Office.
  • Al Ain:  The Main Post Office.
