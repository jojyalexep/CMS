Instant Cash

Emirates Post’s new Instant Cash Global Money Transfer Service offers fast-track money transfer facilities in the UAE. This enables you to send and receive money to many countries worldwide in minutes.

 

Instant Cash Global Money Transfer Service, is a hallmark of speed, convenience, trust and reliability, you can avail this service at any of our post offices between 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

 

How to apply:

  • Fill-up the send or receive form.
  • Give the completed form along with money wish to be sent, along with sender ID and selecting a suitable payout location convenient to the receiver.
  • Giving the ICTC number to the receiver.
  • In minutes, the money will be paid out to the receiver.

 

Instant Cash Global Money Transfer service offers the best exchange rates and the most competitive charges, a complete value for money, with speed, convenience, trust and reliability.
