United States e-Visa Fee Payment

Emirates Post is now authorized to accept the United States e-Visa fees at 82 selected post offices across UAE.

 

How to apply:

  • Proceed to the post office after applying online at http://UAE.usvisa-info.com.
  • Payment of fees should be made within the specified validity period.
  • Payment should be made in cash in UAE dirhams only, the amount is non refundable.
  • The receipt number issued by Emirates Post should be inputted online in the specified space provided on the same website.  
  • To follow up the application please visit: http://UAE.usvisa-info.com.
