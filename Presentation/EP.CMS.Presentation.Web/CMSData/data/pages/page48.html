Tejari BOX

Corporate customers with many trade licenses can rent one P.O Box for all their trade licenses in the same Emirate. 

 

Mail of each company can then be delivered to one P.O. Box, thus saving company’s time and money.  Tejari Box customers should choose a P.O. Box bundle for their main trade licenses, and each additional trade license will be charged as per “P.O . Box Light”.

Subscribers with 5 trade licenses or more shall receive one free mail bag.

Services Features:

  • Registration of all commercial licenses belonging to the same owner for one postal box number, as per the emirate in which the company is registered.
  • Free e-Shaar service.
  • Any number of companies can be registered on the same postal box.
  • Tejari Box can be rented at any of Emirates Post offices.

 

How to apply/renew:

  • Copies of the commercial license should be submitted.
  • Application should have the company stamp.
  • Companies should fill this application in Arabic and English.

Registration is to be done under Tejari P.O. Box number.
