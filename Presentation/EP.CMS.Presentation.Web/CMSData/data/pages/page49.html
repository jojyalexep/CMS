Authorized Agent

Appoint an agent sponsored by the company to receive the incoming mail.

 

How to apply:

  • Provide passport photocopy of the agent with a copy of the residence visa (for expats).
  • Provide 2 Photos (size 4x4.5 cm) of the agent.
  • Complete the Agent Authorization Form, duly signed & stamped.

 

Annual Fee:

the annual fee of this service is AED 100.
