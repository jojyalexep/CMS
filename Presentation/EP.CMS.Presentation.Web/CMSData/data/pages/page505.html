The Department of Economic Development in Ras Al Khaimah hosted a new office for Emirates Post within its headquarters in order to diversify the services provided by the department and to meet the needs of its customers and save their time and effort.

 

Mohamed Al Mahmood, Director of Commercial Affairs at the Department emphasized the importance of Emirates Post office at the headquarters of the Economic Department of Ras Al Khaimah to facilitate the procedures for subscription and renewal of post boxes for holders of commercial licenses at Ras Al Khaimah in particular and the UAE in general, in order to complete the licensing process in less time and effort.

 

Al Mahmood added that the Department is hosting seven offices including the General Headquarters of Ras Al Khaimah Police, Civil Defense, the Courts Department of Ras Al Khaimah, the Office of the Ministry of Labor, Environmental Protection Agency, the General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs in Ras Al Khaimah and the new office of Emirates Post.

 

On his part, Ibrahim Bin Karam, Chief Commercial Officer at Emirates Post Group said that the opening of this new branch in the Department of Economic Development in Ras Al Khaimah is consistent with the keenness of Emirates Post Group to offer better services to the customers and within its strategic plan aimed to expand and develop its network of postal offices spread all over the country.

 

He pointed out that Emirates Post always aim to provide the best level of postal services and support to its individual and corporate customers, and to achieve this goal we are always looking to expand the network of customer service and provide a variety of high quality services.
