Postage Paid

The 'Postage Paid' (PP) facility is available to customers with mail requirements of at least 1,000 Postal letters/items per month or 12,000 Postal letters/items per year, of identical weight and size. 

 

This service permits customers to print 'Postage Paid' on their own envelopes as per their contract with Emirates Post without the need for affixing postage stamps.

 

Service Fees:
- The number of items in the mailing plus, postage charge per item.

- Postal fees will vary according to the weight and destination.

- In some cases, bulk discounts may apply.

Services Features:

Discount rates for local mail will be as per Emirates Post addressing standards, envelop layout and quantities.

 

How to apply:

  • Fill up the "Business Services Subscription form".
  • Copy of the valid Trade License.
  • Passport copy of the owner or the local sponsor.
  • Resident visa copy for the partner non GCC national.
  • Copy of the National ID card.
  • A company signatory must sign the request form.
  • Presenting the original Trade License when applying.
