Business Reply

A company can offer its customers the opportunity to reply to its correspondence, free of charge; respondents simply place their letters in the pre-paid envelope provided and drop it in the nearest post box.  The maximum weight should not exceed 50 grams.

Service Fees:
In addition to an annual AED 750 fee, subscribers pay.

  • AED 1.25 for each returned international response.
  • 50 fils for each response received from within the UAE.

 

Services Features:

  • Customers are more likely to respond as there is no cost to them.
  • Flexible payment.
  • The service spans all locally and 91 countries worldwide.
  • No stipulations are made with regard to the minimum number of replies.
  • It is cost-effective specially on large volume mailings.
  • Payment is only required for the replies received.
  • This service works well with the Unaddressed Mail (to Refer to Marketing Solutions, click here.)

 

How to apply:

  • Fill up the "Business Services Subscription form".
  • A Copy of the company's Trade License and a Post Box in the same name is required.
  • Passport copy of the owner or the local sponsor.
  • Resident visa copy for the partner non GCC national.
  • Copy of the National ID of the applicant.
  • A company signatory must also present the original Trade License to Emirates Post when applying.

 

This service is available locally and internationally to over 90 countries.
