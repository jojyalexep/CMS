Vision:

 

Leadership and innovation in postal, logistics, and financial services, locally and globally.

 

Mission:

 

We work committedly to provide integrated postal and courier services and specialized financial services to individual and corporate customers through optimum use of our human, financial and technical resources according to the highest standards of quality and excellence through our local and global offices network.

2 - Commitment: We pledge to serve our customers, partners and staff along stipulated regulations.


3 - The value of time: We strive to provide services with promptness and above expectations.


4 - Quality and excellence: Our route to provide quality services.


5 - Innovation and creativity: Our way to achieve excellence, exclusivity and leadership.


6 - Team spirit: Our emphasis on the importance of teamwork.


7 - Integrity and transparency: Apply good governance in all our dealings.
