About Emirates Post Group 

Emirates Post Group manages the long-term and medium-term planning of its subsidiaries that consist of Wall Street Exchange Center and Electronic Documents.  The group also manages the two operational units: Emirates Post and Empost.

Emirates Post provides, in addition to core postal services such as Mailing and Parcels, plus a wide range of non-postal services, such as Government services, Financial services, Travel services and Utility Bill Payments services through a network over 115 post offices. 

Empost providers reliable Domestic and International Express Courier services through a fleet of over 550 vehicles and a team of more than 800 specialized staff.

 

Wall Street Exchange

Wall Street Exchange, established in Dubai in 1982, was one of the first companies to provide comprehensive money exchange services in the UAE. In February 2012, Emirates Post Group acquired 100% ownership of the Wall Street Exchange Centre, after buying the remaining 40%. The company has been providing a wide range of foreign exchange, money transfer and related services in the region for over 25 years.

Wall Street’s continued growth has allowed it to set up a worldwide network that includes offices in the UAE, UK and Hong Kong. While the company continues to grow its international presence through agents and partner banks. With its global reach, local expertise and dedicated staff, Wall Street is the preferred choice for currency exchange & remittances. In addition, Wall Street provides other services such as credit card bill and cash payments, National Bonds, Air Arabia, Etisalat and RTA Products, EzeTop and much more.

 

Electronic Documents Centre

Electronic Documents Centre (EDC), a joint venture between Emirates Post Group and ABBA Electronics, is a dedicated facility that handles multiple printing and mailing requirements of large companies.

EDC’s clients include leading financial institutions, loyalty programs and utility companies, which use its hi-tech one-stop shop concept that automates the entire chain from printing to enveloping to mailing, in a secure environment. It operates from its head office at Karama Post Office Building and has a branch in Abu Dhabi.

 

 

Emirates Post  (EP)

 

Emirates Post is one of the leading postal organizations in the Middle East, driven by the mission of becoming a commercially vibrant organization, providing high quality services to the public and the corporate sector, using high technology and innovation.

 

Since its establishment, Emirates Post has attempted to improve and promote its services effectively and achieve higher results in the postal field.

 

Emirates Post has developed new products and services, diversified its offering and established alliances with global and regional postal corporations.

 

Apart from its core offering of postal services, Emirates Post’s diversified portfolio of services today includes: Utility Bill Payment Services, Government Services, Direct Marketing, Banking and Retail Services.
