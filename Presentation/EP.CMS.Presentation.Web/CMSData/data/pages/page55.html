Welcome to the Emirates Post Group (EPG) portal.

Emirates Post Group manages and provides direction to two subsidiaries – Wall Street Exchange and Electronic Documents Centre – and two operating units – Emirates Post and Empost. The subsidiaries and units are supervised by the Group through an approved management structure and clear policies aimed at achieving goals set by the Board of Directors.

EPG allows each subsidiary and unit to work to their full potential, and oversees the management and implementation of investment projects and various business activities.

EPG is responsible for raising the bar on a host of postal and non-postal services offered by the subsidiaries and units. To achieve this goal, we have developed well defined standards, targets and objectives, with a clear focus on employee productivity and highest levels of customer satisfaction.

We are pleased to present a new-look and more user-friendly portal, which reflects the high standards EPG has set for itself and its subsidiaries and units. A lot of planning and innovation has gone into this portal.

We urge all customers and online visitors to browse the main features of the portal and start making use of our e-Services. Needless to say, we are constantly upgrading our online offering, and you will see more value-additions on this portal in the months to come.
