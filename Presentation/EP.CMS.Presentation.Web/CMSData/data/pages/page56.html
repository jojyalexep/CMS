Sealing Permits

For a small annual fee, 'Postage Paid' subscribers can also take advantage of the 'Sealing Permit' service, which allows printed papers to be sealed by special permission from Emirates Post.

How to apply:

  • Fill up the "Business Services Subscription form".
  • Provide a copy of the valid Trade License and a Post Box in the same name is required.
  • Passport copy of the owner or the local sponsor.
  • Resident visa copy for the partner non GCC national.
  • Copy of the National ID card.
  • A company signatory must also present the original Trade License to Emirates Post when applying.
  • At least 1,000 units must be sealed at any given time.

 

Services Features:

The service allows mail to be sent with confidentiality.

 

Service Fee:
The annual subscription fee is AED 200, payable when the contract is signed.
