Franking Machines

Affixing postage stamps by hand is a laborious and time-consuming task; Emirates Post's Franking Machine Service offers companies a fast and efficient alternative. Upon Emirates Post approval permit number will be given to the applicants to purchase a franking machine from any of the registered dealers mentioned below.

 

Mail is then simply fed into the franking machine which automatically prints the value of the postage paid on the top right-hand corner. All postage charges subsequently incurred can then be paid for on credit upon the service contract.

 

Service Conditions:

Receiving a permit number which will allow the purchase a franking machine from one of the company’s authorized by Emirates Post's.

Services Features:

  • Maximizes the efficiency of the subscribing company's outgoing mail as postage values are automatically printed on mail.
  • Stocks of stamps no longer need be kept.
  • Franking can be used to pay for all inland and international mail.
  • An advertising message or logo can be printed alongside the franked impression.

 

Service Fees:
Average cost of franking machine is AED 5,000.

Minimum deposit towards postage costs incurred is AED 1,000.

How to apply / Top UP:

-Send out an official letter requesting for the service.

-Buying the machine for the Authorized Dealers.

- Pay 1000 deposit.

- Fill up the "Business Services Subscription form".

- Brining in the machine for top up and paying the fees up front.
