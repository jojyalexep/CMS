Courier Services

 

The services of Empost are now available at all Emirates Post Offices in the UAE.

The access to Emirates Post offices for cross-selling Empost products provides the national courier company a key platform to take its services to a wider customer base through a network of post offices around UAE.

 

The post offices offer the full range of Empost services through the postal counters, including domestic and international courier services.
