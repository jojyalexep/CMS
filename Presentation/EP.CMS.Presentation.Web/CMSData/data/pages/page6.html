P.O.Box Bundles:

 

1- My Home

Delivering and receiving mail at villa doorstep.

This is exclusively available for villa type houses only in selected areas.

 

Services Features:

  • Delivering and receiving mail at villa doorstep.
  • Accessing the P.O. Box Online (Automated Sorted Ordinary Mail).
  • “eShaar” SMS notification on received registered mail, parcels or express mail.
  • Retaining the P.O. Box number in case change of villa location.
  • Locate P.O. Box through GPS Service.
  • 10% International Courier Service Discount.
  • Temporary suspension of mail pickup and delivery service.

 

Annual Fee:

  • Receiving/ sending mail 3 times a week is AED 750.
  • Receiving/sending mail 6 times a week is AED 1,250.

 

Additional Fees:

  • 70 AED for new P.O. Box registration.
  • Temporary suspension of mail pickup and delivery service.  (per suspension for the value of 100 AED).

 

How to apply/renew:

  • Duly fill up the Individual application form.
  • Two passport photos for new subscriptions.
  • Passport copy and residence visa page for non UAE nationals or copy of National Id card.
  • Copy of the proof of ownership or no objection letter should be submitted.
