Parcels

 

This service is suitable for both small businesses and large corporations. The customer is required to sign a free of charge contract with Emirates Post and benefit from a very cost effective and efficient service. A tracking and tracing system is also included to assure high-reliability service.

 

Services Features:

  • Rates are very reasonable.
  • Payment facilities for contract holders.
  • Large volumes can be sent.

 


How to apply:

  • A contract must be signed between Emirates Post and the organization sending the parcel.
  • A Copy of the company's Trade License and a P.O. Box in subscription under the same name.
  • A company signatory must also present the original Trade License when applying.
  • Each shipment must contain at least five parcels.

 

Service Fees:

  • Postal Parcel charges vary depending on the weight, volume and destination.
  • Parcel senders may make post-dated payments.

 

To check the rates click here.

 

Dimension & Weight Limitations:

  • None of the dimensions of the parcel must exceed 1.5 meters.
  • The max length and greatest circumference must not exceed 3 meters.
  • Maximum weight for international deliveries is 30 kg.
  • Maximum weight within the U.A.E is 50 kg.

 

Type of parcels:

  1. Local Parcel:  Delivery at the Post Office in the U.A.E.
  2. Airmail Parcel:  Parcel to be sent by air.
  3. Surface Parcel:  Surface air lifted (SAL).

For the rates of the service  click here.

 

 

Parcel Boxes:

Strong, light boxes designed to protect large or fragile items whilst they are being transported by post. These boxes are available in four sizes.

 

Pricing:

  • Size 1 (10 x 17.5 x 25cm) : AED 1.50.
  • Size 2 (12 x 25 x 35cm): AED 2.00.
  • Size 3 (15 x 25 x 40cm): AED 3.00.
  • Size 4 (20 x 30 x 50cm): AED 3.50.

Prohibited Articles:

Things that are by their nature hazardous to personnel or can damage the other postal letter items or impeding the implementation of postal services.  It is subject to the rules of the sending and destination country.

 

  1. Arms, ammunition and parts thereof – Aerosol.
  2. Transmitters and radio receivers except as authorized by law.
  3. Pesticides.
  4. Toxic substances.
  5. Political publications.
  6. Gold jewelry, platinum, precious stones and silver, unless sent by insured mail.
  7. Dispatches and parcels in violation of the prescribed packaging conditions.
  8. Lighters full of gas (empty lighters are acceptable).
  9. Radioactive materials.
  10. Pork products and by-products.
  11. Live animals of all kinds.
  12. Drugs and materials that have a psychological impact.
  13. Publications that contain indecent pictures.
  14. Polished, bleached, manufactured or counterfeited pearl.
  15. Flammable films, celluloid or materials manufactured from celluloid.
  16. Flammable, explosive or other hazardous material.
  17. Roots-grain – plants and their parts unless accompanied by a health certificate.
  18. Medical drugs unless accompanied by import license.
  19. Alcoholic beverages.
  20. Publications that are offensive to Arabs and Muslims.
  21. Pornography.
  22. Dispatches and parcels in violation of the prescribed packaging conditions that can be danger to postal workers, or may contaminate or damage the other letters and parcels or postal equipment.
