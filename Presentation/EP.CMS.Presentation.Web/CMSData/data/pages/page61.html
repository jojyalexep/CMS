Purchasable Items

 

1- Parcel Boxes

Strong, light boxes designed to protect large or fragile items whilst they are being transported by post. These boxes are available in four sizes.

 

Pricing:

  • Size 1 (10 x 17.5 x 25cm) : AED 1.50.
  • Size 2 (12 x 25 x 35cm): AED 2.00.
  • Size 3 (15 x 25 x 40cm): AED 3.00.
  • Size 4 (20 x 30 x 50cm): AED 3.50.

 

Service Features:

  • Easy availability of suitable packaging when posting bulky items.
  • Protection of parcel en route, assuring less likelihood of damage to postal items.

 

 

2- Envelopes

Envelope designed to protect the mailed postal item, and it is available in three different sizes.

 

Pricing:

  • Small (9.5cm x 15.5cm): 50 fils.
  • Medium (12cm x 20cm): 75 fils.
  • Large (15cm x 29cm): AED 1.

 

Service Feature:

Emirates Post customers can send registered items safe in the knowledge that they will not be damaged en route.

 

 

3-Aerogramme:

An aerogramme is a single sheet of writing paper that can be folded and glued to form an envelope. No contents may be enclosed in an aerogramme.

 

Pricing:

AED 2 per aerogramme, to anywhere in the world.

 

Service Feature:

The most cost-effective way to send a letter without enclosures to anywhere in the world.
