1- Advertising by Post  (Unaddressed Mail)

Advertising by Post provides direct access to all personal and business P.O Boxes in U.A.E. An extremely cost-effective and flexible mass advertising medium, it is an important element in today's marketing mix.

 

Advertising by Post  is effective both as a standalone medium or in conjunction with Emirates Post's Business Reply Service. 

To refer to Business Reply Service click here.

Service Features:

  • Reaching customers in specific geographic and demographic areas.
  • Helps maintain regular contact with targeted audience at a reasonable cost.
  • Offers access to hundreds of business and personal P.O Boxes.
  • Suitable for the delivery of all types of advertising materials including cards, brochures, tabloids, samples, catalogues, etc..
  • Combining this service  with “Business Reply” helps to view the response rates easily. Also provides the company with the customer data that can be used in building a customer data base.

 

Note: Emirates Post will make every effort to ensure that all material is delivered in the manner requested and within the time frame specified.

 

Service Condition:

  • Items for distribution should not be addressed.
  • Retain permission from the Dubai Economic Department for Dubai only.

2- Promotional Stamp

 

Emirates Post offers a unique opportunity to public and private organizations in the UAE to have special stamps issued as a promotional tool.  This service provides an innovative channel for companies and public corporations to commemorate a special occasion or promote a particular project/message through special stamps issued by Emirates Post. The service can help in enhancing the local and international profile of the organization. And there is the following option:

 

1- Stamps issued in one to two denominations (AED 300,000):

  • 6 Sheets of stamps.
  • 20 Stamp first day covers.
  • 500 Leaflets.
  • 2 Stamp frames.

 

2- Stamps in three or four denominations (AED 350,000):

  • 8 Sheets of stamps.
  • 20 Stamp first day covers.
  • 20 Souvenir sheet first day covers.
  • 1000 Leaflets.
  • 4 Stamp frames.
  • 10 S. Sheets.

 

Services Features:

  • High visibility at the local, regional and international levels.
  • An innovative and effective marketing tool.
  • Reserving for posterity the memory of the occasion.
  • Extra mileage for the issuing company in the local and international market.
  • A means to commemorate the historical event in the country.

 

The package under Promotional Stamps includes:

  • Stamps (sheets)      :  50,000 stamps of each denomination.
  • Souvenir Sheets     :  5000.
  • Leaflets                    : 3000.
  • Stamp First Day Cover:  1000.
  • Souvenir sheet First Day Cover: 500.
  • First Day Cancellation:  2
  • A stamp frame.

 

Technical Details of the stamp:

1- Size:

These are the most suitable sizes for printing stamps.

The sizes can however be altered to suit the applicant’s specific requirements in coordination with Emirates:

  • 30 x 40mm.
  • 40 x 30mm.
  • 40 x 40mm.

 

2- Design:

The design of the proposed stamps will either be provided by the applicant or created by Emirates Post on behalf of the applicant, under mutual consent of  both parties.

 

Value Additions:

  • The stamps will be included in Linder Album (commemorative rare stamps of the UAE from 1973).
  • Media Coverage in local newspapers in the UAE.
  • Display at domestic and international philately exhibitions.
  • Inclusion in Michel and Stanley Gibbons stamp catalogues with other issues of the UAE.
  • Featured on the website of Emirates Post.
  • The stamps will be marketed to member philatelists in and outside the UAE.
  • The stamps will be included in the year-pack of UAE stamps.

 

Conditions for issuing Promotional Stamps:

  • It is exclusive to government and semi government organizations and UAE-registered private companies.
  • Stamp issues are subject to the approval of the Emirates Post Group
  • Subjects selected should meet Emirates Post policies and guidelines

 

 

3- Stamp Imprint Advertising

 

Using stamp cancellation imprints, advertisers and organizations can reach their targeted groups in a novel, measurable and cost-effective fashion. A specially designed stamp cancellation imprint can be designed with any given company's logo or message; this will then be imprinted on mail destined for delivery to hundreds of thousands of households across the UAE and around the world.

Stamping machines providing this service are situated at the Central Post Offices in both Abu Dhabi and Dubai.


Service Fees:
For a fee of AED 50,000 Emirates Post will stamp a company's message on items every day for a three-month period. The company concerned also bears the cost of preparing a cancellation stamp featuring their advertisement or advertising message.


Services Features:

  • Reach is extremely high, in and out of the country.
  • Easy to advertise, particularly when celebrating a special occasion or making a new announcement.
  • Easy to arrange.
