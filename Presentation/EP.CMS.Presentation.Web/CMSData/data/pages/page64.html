Corporate P.O Box

 

Corporate P.O. Box Solutions to suit businesses

Emirates Post offers a choice of Corporate P.O. Box bundles.  Fast, efficient and reliable, with an exciting range of value-added services that are best suited for corporates.

1- P.O.Box Light

 

Services Features:

  • Annual P.O Box rental. 
  • “eShaar” SMS notification on received registered mail, parcels or express mail.
  • 1 Domestic Courier Coupon.
  • 50% discount on Domestic Unaddressed Mail up to 10K units.

 

Annual Fee:

The annual fee for this services is AED 750.

 

Additional Fees:

  • AED 70 for new P.O. Box registration.
  • AED 750 for each additional trade licenses (fill up a form).

 

How to apply/renew:

  • Duly fill up the Corporate application form signed & Stamped.
  • Trade licence copy.
  • Copies of all additional companies trade licenses for Tijari Box Service.
