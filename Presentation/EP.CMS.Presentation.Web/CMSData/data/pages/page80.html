Renting Spaces

 

Emirates Post offers companies wishing to take advantage of Emirates Post expanded network of more than 110 post offices the service booking rented spaces to sell their products and services in post office premises, under different segments, such as:

 

1- Banking and Money Exchange

2- Advertising

3- Business Centers

4- Coffee Shops

5-Other businesses (subject to approval) 

 

 

Service Highlights:

1- Advantage of strategic post-office locations.

2- Easy accessibility.

3- Exposure to company’s products and services to a captive audience

4- Large footfall as customers flock to post offices to avail of multiple services under one roof.

 

 

Annual Rentals:

The rentals will vary according to the:

1- Size of the rented space

2- Location of the Post Office.
