Ordinary Money Order

 

Through Emirates Post's money order service can be sent locally and internationally in a very easy and efficient way to anyone in the world who has access to a post office.

 

After completing a money order form the customer pays the amount specified in U.A.E Dirham’s. Emirates Post will then guarantee prompt same-day dispatch of the transfer to the addressee. The money order is sent to overseas destinations in US Dollars (at a fixed rate of 3.683) and paid out to the addressee in their country's local currency.

Pricing:
AED 3 per transaction plus 0.5% of the value assigned to the destination country, maximum by AED 10.

 

Service Features:

  • Money can be sent anywhere in the world, quickly and reliably.
  • Prompt same-day dispatch guaranteed.
  • Money is paid as per the local currency.
  • Charges are minimal.
  • The service covers regions where other exchange facilities do not exist.
