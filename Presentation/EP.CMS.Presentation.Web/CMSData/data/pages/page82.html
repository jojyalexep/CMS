Individual P.O.Box Terms and Conditions

 

 

1- Renting a P.O.Box

Emirates Post remains the sole owner of the P.O.Boxes.  The P.O.Box should not be sublet or assigned to another individual or entity, without the prior approval of Emirates Post.

 

Emirates Post provides P.O.Box rental service on an annual basis until the end of December. The rental period is calculated from the date of the commencement of subscription.  To be eligible to rent a P.O.Box, customer must be a resident of the UAE.

 

P.O.Box Services Bundles

-          My Home: Delivering and receiving mail at villa doorstep.

-          My Building: Delivering Mail within residential buildings.

-          My Zone: Delivering mail through P.O.Box shelters located in residential and commercial areas.

-          My Box: Delivering mail through P.O.Box at Post Offices

 

2- Your P.O.Box Location

Emirates Post reserves the right to change P.O.Box locations and numbers, after giving one month’s notice.

 

3- Overflow of Mail Items

Mail must be collected regularly. If a P.O.Box overflows with mail, Emirates Post will notify the customers within 15 days, recommending subscription to a Mailbag against annual fees.  Otherwise if the mail over flows again, Emirates Post will charge a fine for managing the overflowed mail. This fine will need to be paid before collecting the accumulated mail. If the fine is not paid for three consecutive months, Emirates Post has the right to cancel the P.O.Box and dispose of the uncollected mail as per the applicable rules and regulations.

 

4- Misuse of P.O.Box

The P.O.Box may not be used for any illegal or fraudulent purposes. The box should not be kept open. Emirates Post shall not be held liable for any loss or damage as a result of such acts or negligence by the subscriber.

 

Emirates Post will be responsible for maintenance and replacement of the box, except in the case of misuse where the customer shall bear the box replacement cost.

 

5- Unknown Mail

In case of unknown mail the customer should drop the same in a box kept for unknown mail.  For “My Home” Service the same should be dropped in the outgoing mail compartment.

 

 

6- Lost Keys

The customer is not allowed to make duplicate keys.   If the P.O.Box keys are lost, the subscriber should apply for a new lock at the post office where his account is held, along with prescribed fees.

 

7- Payment Conditions

Annual P.O.Box rental fees should be paid between December 1st and January 31st. A penalty of AED 100 will be imposed for late payment. Additional AED 10 will be charged for every month after March 1ST.

 

A fine of AED 200 shall be charged for any returned cheque, in addition to the late payment fines mentioned above.

 

Emirates Post has the right to suspend the service if the subscription is not renewed, as per the rules and regulations.

 

8- Withdrawal of P.O.Box service

Emirates Post shall have the right to cancel/withdraw any rented P.O.Box at any time in public interest.

 

9- Upgrading & Downgrading P.O.Box Bundle

A subscriber can upgrade the P.O.Box Bundle at any point throughout the year. He will be charged the pro-rata difference in price. Additional services will also be pro-rated. Downgrade is not allowed during the rental period.

 

10- Value Added Services

 

10-1- Accessing the P.O.Box Online

This service provides flexibility to manage the P.O.Box through online access, viewing the notifications of incoming “eShaar” and photographs of automated sorted ordinary mail. The items displayed online may differ from the actual content of the Mail Box.

 

10-2- SMS notification “eShaar” service

SMS “eShaar” service sends the subscriber a notification upon receiving registered mail, parcel or express mail.

 

This service is free for “My Home”, “My Building”, and “My Zone”.

 

10-3- Retaining the P.O.Box Number

A subscriber may apply for shifting a P.O.Box in the categories )My Home, My Building, My Zone) to a new location within 14 working days against payment of prescribed fees, provided the service is available in the new location and the P.O.Box number is within the range (600000 – 999999). 

 

11- “My Home” service

This is exclusively available for villa type houses in selected locations.

 

11-1- Approval of Landlord

The customer shall be responsible for obtaining the landlord’s approval for P.O.Box installation. Emirates Post shall not assume any responsibility for any dispute arising from installation of the P.O.Box or any damage caused to the building or any part thereof as a result of the P.O.Box installation or removal.


11-2- Locate “My Home” P.O.Box through GPS Service

The GPS coordinates for identifying P.O.Box location are provided upon customer request.

 

 

11-3- International Courier Service Discount

The customer shall get 10% discount on Empost International Courier services, provided that the parcel is sent from the P.O.Box owner’s home or from the Post Office.  The discount shall be valid during the subscription period.

 

11-4- Temporary suspension of mail pickup and delivery service

The customer may apply for temporary suspension of mail pickup and delivery service for a minimum period of one month and maximum period of three months on payment of AED 100 for each application.

 

11-5- Delivery & Pickup

Delivery service of the regular mail letters to the P.O.Box  is available 3 times or 6 times a week, according to customer’s choice.

 

This service includes ordinary mail and small envelopes whose size is compatible with the box capacity and which are free from customs duty. Regarding registered mail and large-size items, a notification shall be dropped in the P.O.Box to enable the customer to collect the same from the post office mentioned in the notification.

 

The postman will collect the outgoing letters (which must have stamps of prescribed value) from the outgoing mail compartment inside “My Home” Mail Box; in case of inadequate stamps, the mail will be returned to the customer. To calculate the required postage stamps, please visit our website: www.emiratespost.ae.

 

Emirates Post shall determine the delivery schedule and shall have the right to change the delivery and pickup timings according to business requirements.

 

12- Cancellation of the P.O.Box service

To enquire about cancellation of the service, please call our call center at 600599999. The subscription fees are not refundable
