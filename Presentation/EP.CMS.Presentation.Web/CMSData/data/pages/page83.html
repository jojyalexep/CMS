Corporate P.O.Box Service Terms and Conditions

 

 

1- Renting a P.O.Box

Emirates Post remains the sole owner of the P.O.Boxes.  The P.O.Box should not be sublet or assigned to another individual or entity, without the prior approval of Emirates Post.

 

Emirates Post provides P.O.Box rental service on an annual basis until the end of December. The rental period is calculated from the date of the commencement of subscription.   To be eligible to rent a P.O.Box, customer must have a valid trade license

 

P.O.Box Services Bundles

1-    P.O Box Light

2-    P.O Box Standard

3-    EziMail Bronze

4-    EziMail Silver

5-    EziMail Gold

 

2- P.O.Box Location

Emirates Post reserves the right to change P.O.Box locations and numbers, after giving one month’s notice.

 

3- Overflow of Mail Items

Mail must be collected regularly. If a P.O.Box overflows with mail, Emirates Post will notify the customers within 15 days, recommending subscription to a Mailbag against annual fees.  If the mail overflows again, Emirates Post will charge a fine for managing the overflowed mail. This fine will need to be paid before collecting the accumulated mail. If the fine is not paid for three consecutive months, Emirates Post has the right to cancel the P.O.Box and dispose of the uncollected mail as per the applicable rules and regulations.

 

4- Misuse of P.O.Box

The P.O.Box should not be used for any illegal or fraudulent purposes. The box should not be kept open. Emirates Post shall not be held liable for any loss or damage as a result of such acts or negligence by the subscriber.

 

Emirates Post will be responsible for maintenance and replacement of the box, except in the case of misuse where the customer shall bear the box replacement cost.

 

5- Unknown Mail

In case of unknown mail the customer should drop the same in a box kept for unknown mail;  otherwise the same should be handed over to the mail pickup and delivery representative. 

 

6- Lost Keys

Customer is not allowed to make duplicate keys.   If the P.O.Box keys are lost, the subscriber should apply for a new lock at the post office where his account is held, along with prescribed fees.

 

7- Payment Conditions

Annual P.O.Box rental fees should be paid between December 1st  and January 31st. A penalty of AED 500 will be imposed for late payment. Additional AED 10 will be charged for every month after March 1ST.

 

A fine of AED 200 shall be charged for any returned cheque, in addition to the late payment fines mentioned above.

 

Emirates Post has the right to suspend the service if the subscription is not renewed, as per the rules and regulations.

 

8- Withdrawal of P.O.Box service

Emirates Post has the right to cancel/withdraw any rented P.O.Box at any time in public interest.

 

9- Upgrading & Downgrading P.O.Box Bundle

A subscriber can upgrade the P.O.Box Bundle at any point throughout the year. He will be charged the pro-rata difference in price. Additional services will also be pro-rated. Downgrade is not allowed during the rental period.

 

10- Value added Services  

10.1- Domestic courier coupons

Coupons will be valid until 31 December and may be used by the customer for courier service up to 5 kgs .The coupons will not be refunded, exchanged or transferred. Additional charges will apply for shipping to remote areas. The virtual coupons are system generated. To use a coupon, please visit the nearest post office or contact the call center at 600599999.

 

10.2- Unaddressed Domestic Mail Offer

To avail of this offer, mail must be sent before 31 December of the subscription year. The domestic mail volume may be used in batches throughout the year with a minimum quantity of 5000 flyers per mailing. Service terms and conditions apply.

 

10.3- SMS Notification “eShaar” Service

SMS “eShaar” service sends the subscriber a notification upon receiving registered mail, parcel or express mail.

 

All of the value added services are valid during the subscription period only.

 

11- EziMail Service

 

11.1- Setting Up The Service.

The new mail pickup and delivery service shall commence within 5 working days after payment of subscription fees. The customer will be informed of the mail letter pickup and delivery time according to the set schedule. Emirates Post shall have the right to change the pickup and delivery schedule.

 

11.2- Pickup & Delivery Mechanism

EziMail Service includes the normal and registered letters.

The customer shall ensure that all letters carry the correct denomination of postage stamps, postage labels or franked impression by an Emirates Post authorized franking machine up to the maximum weight of 5 kgs per pickup.

 

11.3- Change of Address

Please contact our call center on 600599999. Change of address is free within the same city of the P.O.Box; otherwise, additional fees will apply.

 

 12- TIJARI Box

Tijari box customers will choose a bundle for their main trade license. Additional trade licenses will be charged as a P.O.Box Light Bundle, and the subscribers will avail of the value added services for each trade license.

 

The subscribers with 5 trade licenses or more shall receive one free mailbag.

 

13- Cancellation of the P.O.Box service

To enquire about cancellation of the service, please call our call center at 600599999. The subscription fees are not refundable.
